Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my account?

Accounts are created by administrators. You will receive your login details directly from your provider or facility.

What if I did not receive my login credentials?

Please contact your provider or facility administrator to request your account details.

Can I update my personal information?

Updates to your account and medical records must be made by your administrator.

How do I reset my password?

If your account supports password reset, please follow the instructions provided by your administrator.

What should I do if I cannot log in?

Contact your administrator for assistance.

How do I delete my account?

To request deletion of your account or associated data, please contact your administrator. Account deletion requests are handled by the administrator and are subject to legal and healthcare requirements.
