1. Information We Collect

- Account information (created and managed by administrators).
- Personal information (such as name, contact details, and medical data) provided by healthcare providers or administrators on behalf of users.
- Appointment details (date, time, doctor, facility).
- Healthcare facility data (such as hospital names, addresses, and contact information) provided by administrators or authorized facilities.
- Usage data (log data, device information, cookies).

2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide healthcare appointment booking and management services.
- To send notifications (reminders, confirmations, updates).
- To maintain security, improve services, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

- We use encryption and secure infrastructure to protect both user and hospital/facility data.
- Access to personal and medical information is restricted to authorized healthcare providers and administrators.
- We follow healthcare industry best practices to safeguard sensitive information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your information. Data may be shared only:
- With healthcare providers and facilities involved in your care.
- When required by law or regulatory compliance.
- With your consent.

5. Your Rights

Users can:
- Request access to their account and personal information through their healthcare provider or administrator.
- Ask for corrections or updates to their medical data via the administrator.
- Request deletion of their account or associated data, which will be handled by the administrator, subject to legal and healthcare requirements.
- Contact us with privacy concerns at: jithendra@techranges.in

6. Third-Party Content

Healthcare facility information (such as hospital names, addresses, and contact details) displayed in the app is provided by authorized administrators or partners. We do not display or distribute third-party data without the appropriate rights.
